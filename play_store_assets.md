## App title
AnonPro — Anonymous Social

## Short description
Anonymous social app for confessions, chats, and community posts.

## Full description
AnonPro is an anonymous‑first social platform built for honest expression and safer community interactions. Post confessions, share updates, and join topic‑based rooms with privacy controls. Chat with individuals or groups, browse stories, and discover community trends.

Features:
- Anonymous posts and confession rooms
- Direct and group messaging
- Posts with images and tags
- Push notifications with quiet hours
- Community guidelines, blocking, and reporting

We prioritize user safety with moderation tools and abuse‑prevention systems.
